## Core Attributes for the Isagi Build

In Locked 2, your physical stats determine your baseline performance before skills even come into play. For a **locked 2 isagi build**, you aren't looking to be the tallest or the strongest player on the pitch. Instead, you need a balance of agility and endurance. Isagi’s playstyle is built on off-the-ball movement, which requires a high stamina pool and enough speed to find open space.

## Essential Weapons and Skills

The heart of any **locked 2 isagi build** lies in the weapons you equip. In Locked 2, weapons are divided into tiers, with Legendary and Epic skills providing the most significant advantages. To truly embody Isagi, you must aim for skills that enhance your vision and your ability to score without taking unnecessary touches.

### Meta Vision (Legendary)
This is the cornerstone of the build. Meta Vision allows you to see the entire field's layout, highlighting player positions and potential passing lanes. In the 2026 meta, this skill provides a passive buff to your reaction time, making it easier to intercept balls and predict where a loose ball will land.

### Direct Shot (Epic/Legendary)
Isagi’s signature finishing move. In Locked 2, the Direct Shot weapon allows you to fire a powerful, accurate shot immediately after receiving a pass or landing a trap. This minimizes the window for defenders to tackle you (Q key) and increases the goal conversion rate significantly.

### Stealthy Steps or Monster Lift
While Isagi isn't a primary dribbler, having "Stealthy Steps" allows you to move through the defense undetected, which is perfect for poacher-style gameplay. Alternatively, "Monster Lift" can be used as a secondary weapon to bypass aggressive goalkeepers who rush out of the box.

## Mastering the Flow State

The Flow State in Locked 2 is a game-changer for the **locked 2 isagi build**. When your G-bar is full, entering Flow (G key) grants you a temporary surge in all physical attributes. For an Isagi-type player, this is the moment where "spatial awareness" reaches its peak. 

During Flow, your "puzzle pieces" VFX will activate, and your ability to land a Direct Shot becomes almost guaranteed if you are within the final third of the pitch. However, managing the drain rate is crucial. You should choose a Flow type that offers a "Slower Drain" even if the buff is slightly weaker, as Isagi builds thrive on sustained pressure rather than a single 5-second burst.

## Tactical Gameplay and Controls

To succeed with a **locked 2 isagi build**, you must master the control scheme. Locked 2 uses a hybrid system of legacy and beginner dribbling. For this specific build, "Legacy Dribbling" is often preferred by high-level players because it allows for more precise ball placement when setting yourself up for a shot.

1.  **Positioning:** Stay near the edge of the offside line. Use Meta Vision to identify which defender has the lowest stamina.
2.  **The Interception:** Use M2 (Steal) and Q (Tackle) sparingly. Isagi builds should focus on intercepting passes rather than physical duels.
3.  **The Finish:** When the ball comes to you, hold Right-Click (M2) to charge your shot while the ball is still in the air. Timing the release as the ball reaches your feet triggers the Direct Shot mechanic.
4.  **Stamina Management:** Always keep at least 20% of your stamina bar full. If you hit zero, your character will enter a "huffing" animation, leaving you vulnerable.

> **⚠️ Warning:** Do not over-rely on dribbling. The Isagi build is designed for "One-Touch" soccer. If you hold the ball for more than three seconds, you are likely playing the build incorrectly.

## FAQ

**Q: What is the best secondary weapon for a Locked 2 Isagi build?**

A: While Direct Shot is your primary, "Stealthy Steps" is an excellent secondary. It allows you to slip past defenders' peripheral vision, making it much easier to find the open space needed for Isagi's signature finishes.

**Q: Can I use the Isagi build for 1v1 matches?**

A: The **locked 2 isagi build** is primarily designed for team play (3v3 or higher). In 1v1s, you may struggle against builds with high dribbling stats like "Monster Lift" or "Serpent," as Isagi relies on off-the-ball movement and passing.

**Q: How do I activate Meta Vision in-game?**

A: Meta Vision is usually mapped to the 'R' or 'T' key depending on your weapon slot. Once activated, your screen may have a slight blue tint or VFX change, and you will see player trajectories more clearly.

**Q: Is speed more important than power for Isagi?**

A: Yes. In Locked 2, Isagi's effectiveness comes from being in the right place at the right time. As long as you have enough power to beat the goalkeeper, your priority should always be speed and stamina to maintain your positioning throughout the match.
