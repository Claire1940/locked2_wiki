## The Core Philosophy of a Midfielder Build

In the high-stakes environment of Locked 2, the midfielder is the engine of the team. Unlike strikers who focus solely on finishing or defenders who prioritize physicality, a midfielder must be a jack-of-all-trades. The primary goal of a **locked 2 midfielder build** is to maintain ball possession and create scoring opportunities while disrupting the opponent’s momentum.

To achieve this, players often look toward builds that offer a mix of "Curve" potential and "Counter" mechanics. By balancing these elements, you become a threat from distance while remaining an obstacle for any striker attempting to dribble through the center.

## Stat Allocation and Progression

Building your character in Locked 2 is a marathon, not a sprint. For a **locked 2 midfielder build**, your stat points should be invested carefully to avoid "empty stats" that don't contribute to your role. In 2026, the community has found that over-investing in power at the expense of technique leads to a build that is easily intercepted.

### Recommended Leveling Path
1.  **Levels 1-20:** Focus heavily on **Stamina** and **Ball Control**. You need to be able to stay on the pitch and keep the ball under pressure.
2.  **Levels 21-40:** Begin investing in **Technique/Passing**. This unlocks the full potential of weapons like Riptide.
3.  **Levels 41+:** Fine-tune your **Speed** and **Defense** stats based on whether you prefer a more Offensive Midfielder (CAM) or Defensive Midfielder (CDM) role.

| Level Tier | Focus Area | Goal |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Early Game** | Foundation | Maximize Stamina to 40+ points. |
| **Mid Game** | Skill Expression | Raise Ball Control and Technique to enable Riptide curves. |
| **End Game** | Specialization | Distribute remaining points into Speed for better recovery. |

## Mastering the Riptide Weapon

The Riptide weapon is often misunderstood as a purely offensive tool. However, in the hands of a skilled midfielder, it becomes a versatile utility. The "Curve Shot" mechanic allows you to bypass the defensive line entirely. 

If you are playing the center-mid position, you can use the Riptide to "swing" the ball from the left flank to the right flank with a single input. This forces the opposing defense to shift rapidly, often creating gaps for your teammates to exploit.

### Perfect Counter Timing
The **Perfect Counter** is the defensive backbone of this **locked 2 midfielder build**. It requires precise timing. If you trigger it too early, you leave yourself vulnerable to a dribble. If you trigger it too late, the opponent has already passed you.

> **Warning:** Relying too heavily on the counter can make your playstyle predictable. Mix in manual interceptions to keep the opponent guessing.

## Advanced Midfield Tactics

To truly excel with a **locked 2 midfielder build**, you must understand the concept of "Space Management." You are the bridge between the defense and the attack. 

### Defensive Positioning
As a midfielder, your job isn't always to tackle the ball. Often, your job is simply to cut off the passing lanes. Use your speed to shadow the opposing playmaker. If they make a mistake or attempt a risky dribble, that is when you engage your Perfect Counter.

### Offensive Transition
Once you regain possession, don't immediately sprint toward the goal. Look for your strikers. A well-placed curve pass using Riptide can put your striker in a 1v1 situation with the goalkeeper. This "pass-first" mentality is what separates top-tier midfielders from average players.

| Situation | Action | Expected Outcome |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Opponent Dribbling** | Shadow & Counter | Turnover and immediate possession. |
| **Crowded Midfield** | Riptide Curve Pass | Bypasses the crowd to find open wings. |
| **Loose Ball** | Stamina Sprint | Secures possession before the opponent recovers. |

## FAQ

**Q: Is the Riptide weapon better than the standard Power weapon for midfielders?**

A: In the current 2026 meta, Riptide is generally preferred for midfielders. While Power weapons help with long-range goals, the Riptide curve shot provides much better utility for playmaking and bypassing defenders, which is the primary role of a **locked 2 midfielder build**.

**Q: How many points should I put into Defense for a CAM build?**

A: For a Central Attack Midfielder (CAM), you can keep Defense relatively low (around 15-20 points) and focus more on Technique and Ball Control. However, if you are playing a more balanced or defensive role, you should aim for 35+ points in Defense to make your interceptions more effective.

**Q: Can I use the Toshiro build for other positions?**

A: While specifically optimized for the midfield, the Toshiro build can function as a secondary striker (CF). However, its defensive capabilities like the Perfect Counter are most valuable in the middle of the pitch where the most action occurs.

**Q: What is the most important stat for a locked 2 midfielder build?**

A: Stamina is arguably the most important. A midfielder who runs out of energy becomes a liability, as they can no longer track back to defend or push forward to support the attack. Always prioritize a healthy stamina pool before maximizing your offensive stats.
