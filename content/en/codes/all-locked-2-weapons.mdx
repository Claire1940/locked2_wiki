## Offensive Weapons: Shooting and Scoring

The primary goal in Locked 2 is to find the back of the net. Offensive weapons are designed to bypass the goalkeeper through raw power, unpredictable curves, or sheer speed. In the current 2026 season, precision and timing have become more important than ever.

### Powerful Strike Abilities
These weapons focus on the velocity and trajectory of the ball. Unlike standard shots, these abilities often have unique modifiers that make them difficult to track.

| Skill Name | Description | Key Feature |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Emperor Impact** | A high-velocity, long-range shot. | **No hold time** required; instant activation. |
| **Big Bang Drive** | A versatile 180° shot with vertical control. | **Adjustable flight height** for varied angles. |
| **Riptide Curve** | A shot that allows the player to control the curve direction. | High **unpredictability** for goalkeepers. |

> **💡 Tip:** You can combine specific shots, like Emperor Impact, with other mechanics to create a "Magnus Shot," which offers extreme speed and erratic movement.

### Aerial and Header Weapons
Not every goal is scored with the feet. Aerial dominance is a vital part of the **all locked 2 weapons** catalog, especially for players who prefer a physical presence in the box.

*   **Dragon Header:** This is an aggressive aerial move where the player launches themselves forward. It is ideal for meeting crosses early and catching the defense off guard.
*   **Crushing Header:** A simpler but more powerful version of a standard header. It provides a significant boost to the ball's downward velocity, making it hard for goalkeepers to react in time.

## Defensive and Goalkeeping Weapons

A strong offense wins games, but a strong defense wins championships. The defensive weapons in Locked 2 are designed to expand your reach and neutralize the most dangerous strikers.

### Defensive Utility
*   **Serpent:** This is a versatile defensive skill that allows you to adjust the height of your jump. It is perfect for intercepting high lobs or blocking "Big Bang Drive" attempts.
*   **Stylish Reach:** This passive-style weapon extends your defensive range. It effectively gives your character a larger hitbox, making it easier to poke the ball away during a tackle.
*   **Bunny:** A simple but effective utility that ensures your next jump is significantly higher. This is essential for winning 50/50 balls in the air.

### Goalkeeper (GK) Specifics
Goalkeepers have access to specialized weapons that can turn the tide of a match. If you are playing this role, you must familiarize yourself with these tools.

| Weapon | Function | Advantage |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Bear Claw** | Catching | Allows the GK to **snatch the ball** instead of deflecting it. |
| **Primal Handstand** | Blocking | Provides a **massive hitbox** for a short duration. |
| **Black Hole Trap** | Control | Ability to **catch the ball mid-air** and hold it securely. |

> **⚠️ Warning:** While "Primal Handstand" provides a huge defensive area, its duration is short. Miscalculating the timing can leave the goal wide open.

## Strategic Awareness: Meta Vision

Among **all locked 2 weapons**, "Meta Vision" stands out as perhaps the most powerful utility in the game. It is not a physical strike or a tackle, but a cognitive boost that alters how you perceive the match.

When Meta Vision is active, the player gains several significant advantages:
1.  **Trajectory Visualization:** You can see the projected path of the ball, allowing for perfect interceptions.
2.  **Speed Boost:** A passive increase to movement speed helps you reposition across the pitch.
3.  **Ankle Break Immunity:** You become impossible to "ankle break," meaning dribblers cannot stun you with flashy moves.

Using Meta Vision effectively requires high stamina management, but it is the hallmark of a top-tier player in the 2026 competitive circuit.

## Tactical Combinations and Synergy

To truly master **all locked 2 weapons**, you must understand how they interact with one another. A player who only uses one skill is predictable. A player who chains them is unstoppable.

### The "Speed Demon" Build
This build focuses on "Immense Speed" and "Stealthy Steps." By activating stealth at the start of a surge, you can reposition behind the defense without being tracked. This is often followed by a "Direct Shot" to finish the play.

### The "Impenetrable Wall" Build
For defenders, combining "Stylish Reach" with "Serpent" allows you to cover both ground and air. If a striker attempts to dribble past, "Stylish Reach" ensures your tackle connects from a further distance than they expect.

### The "Ultimate Striker" Build
Striker builds often revolve around "Meta Vision" to find the gap in the defense, followed by an "Emperor Impact." Because Emperor Impact requires no hold time, it can be fired the moment a gap is visualized through Meta Vision.

## FAQ

**Q: What is the best weapon for a beginner in Locked 2?**

A: For beginners, the **Power Shot** or **Crushing Header** are excellent choices. They are straightforward to use and help you understand the basic physics of the game before moving on to more complex weapons like Meta Vision.

**Q: How do I perform a Magnus Shot?**

A: A Magnus Shot is typically a combination of **Emperor Impact** and specific directional inputs or secondary skills that add erratic movement to the ball, making it nearly impossible for a GK to predict.

**Q: Can I use multiple weapons at once?**

A: While you can have multiple skills equipped, most require individual activation. However, passive weapons like **Stylish Reach** are always active, and some skills like **Meta Vision** provide buffs that last while you use other abilities.

**Q: Which weapon is best for Goalkeepers?**

A: **Bear Claw** is widely considered the best for GKs because it allows you to maintain possession of the ball after a save, rather than deflecting it back into the path of an opposing striker. Among **all locked 2 weapons**, it offers the highest level of defensive security.
