### Effective Defensive Lineups
A common mistake for newer teams is neglecting the backline. In Locked 2, having at least two dedicated defenders with the "Serpent" weapon can neutralize even the fastest strikers. 

1.  **The Box Defense**: Position two defenders inside the penalty area to assist the GK against "bomb shots."
2.  **The Midfield Press**: Use a CDM (Central Defensive Midfielder) to intercept passes before they reach the box.
3.  **The Wing Back Trap**: Force attackers toward the sidelines where their shooting angles are limited.

> **⚠️ Warning:** Relying too heavily on AI Goalkeepers can be dangerous in high-ping situations. Many competitive leagues now mandate human GKs to ensure fair play and higher skill ceilings.

## Overcoming Technical Challenges: Ping and Server Locality

Because Locked 2 is a global game, players often face the challenge of high latency (ping). For players in Asia or Europe playing on North American servers, a ping of 250ms or higher is not uncommon. This latency affects "one-touch" shots and defensive timing significantly.

To compensate for high ping, players should:
*   **Predict, Don't React**: Start your defensive animations slightly before the opponent reaches you.
*   **Avoid One-Touches**: On high latency, the game may not register a one-touch shot correctly. Trap the ball first if space allows.
*   **Focus on Positioning**: If you cannot rely on twitch reflexes, focus on being in the right place to intercept passes automatically.

| Ping Range | Gameplay Impact | Recommended Strategy |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **0 - 60ms** | Minimal | Aggressive 1v1 dribbling and reflex saves |
| **60 - 150ms** | Moderate | Focus on passing and tactical spacing |
| **150ms+** | Severe | Defensive "Meat Wall" roles or predictive striking |
