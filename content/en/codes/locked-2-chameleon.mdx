## Understanding the Chameleon Copy Mechanic

The core of the Chameleon Copy weapon lies in its "Copycat" nature. Unlike static weapons that provide a single set of skills, this weapon is entirely dependent on the other players in your current match. If an opponent is using a specific weapon like "Bunnies" or "Power Shot," you have the potential to replicate those abilities for your own use.

The activation of the **locked 2 chameleon** is strictly tied to individual ball possession. It does not matter if your team has the ball; what matters is whether the ball is currently at your feet.

| Possession Status | Resulting Weapon Type | Example Skills Copied |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Individual Possession** | **Offensive Weapon** | Bunnies, Power Shot, Scissors, Nagi Skills |
| **No Possession** | **Defensive Weapon** | Stylish Reach, Stealthy Steps, Butcher Moves |

> **💡 Tip:** Before activating your copy skill, take a quick look at the player list or observe the field. You can only copy weapons that are actually equipped by other players in that specific session.

## Managing Cooldowns and Rarity

The **locked 2 chameleon** is a Legendary weapon, meaning it is quite rare to roll in the customization menu. Because of its high tier, the cooldowns associated with it are significant. Generally, you can expect a base cooldown of approximately 60 to 80 seconds.

It is important to note that the cooldown duration may vary slightly depending on the specific weapon you copy. More powerful offensive skills often trigger a longer wait time before you can use Chameleon Copy again.

### Rarity Confirmation Settings
In the customization tab, players can set filters to prevent accidentally rolling over high-rarity weapons. Since Chameleon is a Legendary, you should ensure your settings are configured correctly.

1.  **The Bottom Corner Meta:** In current "Autos" matches, the AI Goalkeepers often struggle with shots aimed at the bottom corners. If you copy an offensive weapon with a shooting buff, aim for these "cripple zones" for a nearly guaranteed goal.
2.  **Ping Sensitivity:** Copying weapons like "Scissors" or high-speed dribbles is much more effective on lower ping. If you are playing on high-latency servers, focus on copying defensive "Reach" skills which are more forgiving.

## FAQ

**Q: Can I choose which weapon I copy with the Locked 2 Chameleon?**

A: No, the weapon you receive is randomly selected from the pool of weapons currently equipped by other players in your match. However, you can control whether you get an offensive or defensive weapon based on ball possession.

**Q: Does the Chameleon weapon copy the stats of the other player?**

A: No, it only copies the weapon's skills and animations. Your own base stats (Speed, Power, etc.) still apply to the copied moves, which is why your build still matters even when using a copy weapon.

**Q: What happens if no one else in the game has a weapon equipped?**

A: If there are no weapons to copy, the Chameleon ability will not function correctly. However, in almost all public and Autos matches, you will find a variety of weapons to pull from.

**Q: Is the 60-second cooldown fixed for every copy?**

A: The base cooldown is roughly 60 seconds, but in practice, it can range up to 80 seconds depending on the complexity and power of the weapon you have successfully replicated. Private servers with multipliers may also alter these times.
