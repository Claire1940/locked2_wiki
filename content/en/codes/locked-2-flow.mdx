## Locked 2 Weapon Tier List: S-Tier Dominance

In the world of Locked 2, S-Tier weapons are those that can single-handedly change the outcome of a match. These abilities often possess "I-frames" (invincibility frames) or hitboxes so large they are nearly impossible to counter without specific high-tier defensive tools.

| Weapon Name | Rarity | Type | Best For |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Emperor Impact** | Unique | Offensive | Strikers / Wingers |
| **Serpent** | Unique | Defensive | Center Backs |
| **Bunnies** | Rare | Defensive | Fullbacks / Midfielders |
| **Big Bang Drive** | Unique | Offensive | Long-range Scorers |

> **💡 Tip:** If you pull a Unique weapon like Emperor Impact, do not spin off it. The cost of currency in Locked 2 is high, and these weapons are statistically superior to almost everything else in the game.

### Emperor Impact
Based on the legendary Kaiser from the manga, Emperor Impact is widely considered the best offensive weapon for achieving a consistent **locked 2 flow**. It allows for a hyper-powered shot that can be activated with a simple button press. When combined with other modifiers like Riptide Curve, it creates the "Magna Shot," a nearly unblockable projectile that maintains incredible speed and trajectory.

### Serpent
Serpent is the gold standard for defenders. Its hitbox is massive, allowing players to clear balls and intercept shots that would otherwise be out of reach. In competitive 11v11 scrims, having a backline equipped with Serpent is often the difference between a clean sheet and a blowout.

## A-Tier: High Utility and Reliable Meta Options

A-Tier weapons are excellent choices that provide specific advantages. While they may require more skill to execute than S-Tier "auto-win" buttons, they are highly respected in the competitive community.

| Weapon Name | Rarity | Primary Effect | Strategy |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Immense Speed** | Legendary | Burst Speed | Best for wingers to outrun defenders. |
| **Crushing Header** | Epic | Aerial Power | Use on lobs for an Emperor-level headshot. |
| **Riptide Curve** | Legendary | Ball Trajectory | Essential for curving shots around GKs. |
| **Black Hole Trap** | Epic | Ball Control | Auto-locks the ball to you for easy setups. |

### Immense Speed and Positioning
Immense Speed functions by providing bursts of velocity over a 15-second window. To maximize the **locked 2 flow** with this weapon, you must time your activation during a counter-attack. While the max speed only lasts for a few seconds, it is more than enough to collect a loose ball or bypass a flat-footed defender.

### The Power of Crushing Header
This is a specialized weapon that shines in a coordinated team. When a teammate lobs the ball into the box, Crushing Header activates a shot with power comparable to an Emperor Impact but delivered from the air. It is particularly effective in Autos where goalkeepers often struggle with vertical timing.

## Defensive Specialists and Goalkeeping

Defending in Locked 2 requires more than just standing in front of the goal. You need weapons that extend your reach or provide physical dominance over the ball.

| Defensive Weapon | Tier | Key Advantage |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Primal Handstand** | GK Only | Broken hitbox that covers top corners. |
| **Stylish Reach** | Rare | Extended M2 hitbox for easier steals. |
| **Bunnies** | Rare | Superior aerial interception. |
| **Bear Claw** | Common | Reliable base-level goalkeeping. |

> **⚠️ Warning:** Primal Handstand is so powerful that many competitive leagues have banned its use by non-goalkeepers. If you are playing in a private server scrim, always check the rules regarding GK weapons.

## B-Tier and C-Tier: Niche and Falling Off

As the game evolves, some classic weapons have fallen out of favor. These are generally "all right" but lack the overwhelming presence of the higher tiers.

*   **Power Shot (Rare):** Good for clearing the ball from the defensive third, but the charge time is too long for high-pressure offensive situations.
*   **Direct Shot (Common):** Once a meta-defining skill, it now requires perfect timing and a teammate's pass, making it too situational for most players.
*   **Metavision (Unique):** While it provides a speed boost and trajectory lines, its long cooldown and short duration make it the weakest Unique weapon in the 2026 landscape.
*   **Super Fast Scissors (Epic):** Provides some I-frames during stepovers, but the dash at the end leaves you vulnerable to steals.

## The "Chameleon" Factor: High Risk, High Reward

Chameleon is a Legendary weapon that defies standard tier lists. It allows you to copy a random offensive weapon when you have the ball or a defensive weapon when you don't. Because it can copy S-Tier abilities like Emperor Impact or Serpent, it has the potential to be the best weapon in the game. However, it can also copy low-tier skills like Stealthy Steps, making it inherently unpredictable.

To succeed with Chameleon and maintain your **locked 2 flow**, you must be proficient with every weapon's mechanics. You never know what you will get, so adaptability is your greatest asset.

## How to Maximize Your Weapon Potential

1.  **Synergize with Stats:** If you use Immense Speed, ensure your base Speed stat is high to make the multiplier even more effective.
2.  **Learn the "Tech":** Many weapons have hidden mechanics. For example, Riptide Curve has a "Straight Riptide" tech that allows for a power shot without the curve.
3.  **Manage Your Wallet:** Getting money in Locked 2 is notoriously difficult. If you have an A-Tier weapon, it is often better to keep it and upgrade your stats rather than gambling for an S-Tier.
4.  **Practice in Private Servers:** Use [official Roblox private servers](https://www.roblox.com) to practice 11v11 scrims where you can learn how weapons interact in a lag-free environment.

## FAQ

**Q: What is the absolute best weapon for a beginner in Locked 2?**

A: If you are lucky enough to pull it, **Emperor Impact** is the most beginner-friendly offensive weapon. For defenders, **Bunnies** is highly recommended because it simplifies the process of winning aerial duels.

**Q: Why is Monster Lift ranked so low in the 2026 meta?**

A: Monster Lift lacks I-frames (invincibility frames). In high-level play, a defender can simply jump up and "chest trap" the ball while you are performing the move, making it a liability rather than an asset for your **locked 2 flow**.

**Q: Can I use goalkeeper weapons like Primal Handstand as a striker?**

A: Technically, yes, but it is highly discouraged. Goalkeeper weapons are designed for shot-blocking. Using them on offense wastes your weapon slot, and in competitive "Scrim" environments, it is often against the rules for field players to use GK-specific abilities.

**Q: How do I activate the "Magna Shot"?**

A: The Magna Shot is a combination of Emperor Impact, Power Shot, and Riptide Curve. It requires specific weapon stacking and timing to execute correctly, resulting in the most powerful shot currently available in the game.
