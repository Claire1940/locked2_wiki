## Getting Started: Matchmaking and Social Setup

Before diving into the specific **locked 2 ps commands**, you must ensure your server is configured for the right audience. High latency can ruin a soccer match, making timing and "Flow" activation nearly impossible. 

### Selecting Your Region
The first step for any host is to set the matchmaking region. This ensures that all players connecting to your session experience the lowest possible ping. To change this, navigate to the "Other" tab in the main menu and select "Matchmaking Region." In 2026, the available regions include:
*   North America (NA)
*   South America (SA)
*   Europe (EU)
*   Asia
*   Oceania

### Managing Your Party
If you are preparing for a team-based session, use the social list on the right side of the screen. You can see player statuses, including whether they are solo, in a duo, or part of a larger group (up to four players). To invite a friend, simply click the "Plus" icon next to their name. If you find yourself being overwhelmed by requests, you can toggle "Party Invites" off in the settings menu to maintain your privacy while configuring your server.

## Essential Locked 2 PS Commands

The heart of private server management lies in the "Commands" tab within the main menu. This interface allows you to toggle global server settings that affect every player on the pitch.

| Command | Function | Recommended Setting |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Server Lock** | Prevents new players from joining the session. | **On** for private scrims. |
| **Boss Spawning** | Enables or disables the ability to spawn boss-level entities. | **Off** for competitive play. |
| **Cooldowns** | Toggles skill and ability cooldown timers. | **Off** for intensive practice. |
| **Reset Ball** | Instantly teleports the ball back to the center mark. | Use as needed. |
| **Give Flow** | Grants the "Flow" state to a specific player or yourself. | Great for testing ultimates. |

> **💡 Tip:** If you are on PC, the fastest way to spawn a ball is by pressing the **B** key. This shortcut is the universal standard for "Ball" and saves you from navigating menus mid-drill.

## Match Management and Scoreboard Controls

For those hosting tournaments or organized matches, the Match section of the **locked 2 ps commands** is your primary toolkit. This section controls the flow of time and the tracking of points.

### Clock and Whistle Controls
The game clock is fully manual in private servers. You have the option to "Play" or "Pause" the timer at any moment. This is particularly useful for coaching sessions where you might want to stop the action to explain a tactical positioning error. Additionally, the "Whistle" command can be used to signal fouls or the end of a half, providing an immersive referee experience.

### Field Customization
Not every match requires a massive stadium. Locked 2 allows you to toggle between "Small" and "Large" field sizes. Small fields are excellent for 2v2 or 3v3 "Street" style matches, while the Large field is the standard for professional 11v11 play.

| Feature | Description | Best Use Case |

| **Reset Scoreboard** | Clears all goals and resets the match to 0-0. | New Match Starts |

## Admin and Player Moderation

As a server owner, you have "Admin" authority over everyone in the instance. This is vital for removing disruptive players or helping someone who has glitched through the map.

The following actions can be applied to any player currently on the server list:
1.  **Admin:** Grants another player access to the command menu.
2.  **Referee Mode:** Puts a player into a neutral state to oversee the match.
3.  **Kick:** Removes a player from the current session.
4.  **Blacklist:** Permanently bans a player from re-joining your private server.
5.  **Teleport:** Moves a player to your current coordinates (useful for gathering teams).
6.  **Freeze:** Roots a player in place, preventing movement or ability usage.

> **Warning:** Use the Blacklist command sparingly. In the Locked 2 community, blacklisting players without cause can result in a poor reputation among the competitive leagues.

## Advanced Features: AI Bots and Team Creation

One of the most impressive updates in 2026 is the inclusion of AI Goalkeepers (GK). If you are practicing your shooting but don't have a friend to play goalie, you can spawn an AI assistant.

### Spawning an AI Goalkeeper
To set this up, walk to the goal area where you want the bot to stand. Open the main menu, navigate to "Bots," and select "Place." An AI GK will appear instantly. These bots are surprisingly capable and will react to shots based on the current server modifiers. If you are assigned to a team, the AI will recognize you as an ally and can even distribute the ball back to you after a save.

### Team Customization

## FAQ

**Q: How do I spawn a ball using locked 2 ps commands on mobile?**

A: While PC players can use the "B" key, mobile and console players must open the Main Menu, navigate to the "Commands" tab, and manually click the "Spawn Ball" or "Reset Ball" button. There is currently no dedicated touch-gesture for ball spawning in the 2026 build.

**Q: Can I save my team settings in a private server?**

A: Currently, team names and custom configurations reset once the private server instance closes. It is recommended to keep a notepad with your preferred settings and team names to quickly re-input them when starting a new session.

**Q: What do the "Multipliers" actually change?**

A: Multipliers function as gameplay modifiers. They can increase the speed of the ball, the gravity of the world, or the power of your character's kick. Most competitive private servers keep these at 1.0 to mirror the official Ranked experience.

**Q: Is there a way to prevent people from using flow in my server?**

A: Yes, you can effectively disable Flow by turning "Cooldowns" on and setting the Flow gain multiplier to zero if the specific server version supports it. Alternatively, you can use the "Referee Mode" to manually strip Flow from players who use it during restricted practice sessions.
