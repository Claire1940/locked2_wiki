## Advanced Gameplay Tactics

Success in this game isn't just about having the best traits; it’s about how you use them. The gameplay is designed to punish selfish "egoists" who refuse to pass.

### The Art of the Fake Shot
By pressing the designated "Fake" key (often T or a specific mouse bind) during a shot charge, you can bait the opposing defender into a slide tackle. Once they are on the ground, you have a clear lane to either pass or finish the play. This is a core mechanic for breaking down high-level defenses.

### Stamina Management
Stamina is the most precious resource in any match. Sprinting constantly will leave you "naked" (figuratively) when you actually need to make a burst toward the goal. 

*   **Conserve during buildup:** Walk or use light jogs while the ball is in your own half.
*   **Burst in the final third:** Use your full stamina only when you are breaking the defensive line.
*   **Cooldowns:** Be aware that your special weapons have cooldowns. Don't waste a "Stealthy Step" burst when you are already marked by two defenders.
