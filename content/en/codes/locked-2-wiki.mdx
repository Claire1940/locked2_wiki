## Mastering Gameplay Mechanics and Controls

### Basic Control Scheme
*   **WASD:** Movement
*   **Left Click (M1):** Basic Shot / Tackle
*   **Right Click (M2):** Power Shot / Heavy Tackle
*   **E:** Sprint
*   **Q:** Dribble Skill / Shift Lock Toggle
*   **Space:** Jump / Header

### The Flow State
The "Flow State" is a unique mechanic that triggers when a player performs a series of successful actions, such as consecutive passes, successful dribbles, or goals. When in Flow, your stats receive a temporary boost, and your character may gain access to exclusive "Flow Skills" that can turn the tide of a match.

> **⚠️ Warning:** Entering the Flow State also increases your Stamina consumption. Use this boost wisely to secure a goal rather than wasting it in your own half.

## Advanced Tactical Strategies

Once you have mastered the basics, you must begin thinking about the tactical side of the game. Professional teams in Locked 2 do not just run toward the ball; they maintain formations and use "Zonal Marking" to shut down opponents.

*   **Triangle Passing:** Always look for two passing options to create a triangle. This makes it incredibly difficult for a single defender to intercept the ball.
*   **Through Balls:** Instead of passing directly to a teammate, pass into the space in front of them. This allows them to maintain their momentum and beat the defensive line.
