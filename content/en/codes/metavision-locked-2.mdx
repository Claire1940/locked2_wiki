## Understanding the Metavision Locked 2 Meta

The **metavision locked 2** unique weapon is often misunderstood by newer players who see its "Unique" rarity and assume it is an automatic top-tier pick. While it offers several utility-based buffs, it currently sits in the C-Tier for competitive play. Its primary functions include a noticeable speed boost, the ability to see the ball's trajectory, and a passive resistance to being "ankle broken" by flashy dribblers.

While these sounds like powerful tools, the reality of the 2026 meta favors high-impact, immediate-result weapons. Metavision lasts for approximately 30 seconds but suffers from a lengthy cooldown that leaves you vulnerable for large portions of the match. In "Autos," it can help you intercept passes and navigate through chaotic midfields, but in organized scrims, more specialized weapons like Serpent or Emperor Impact provide much higher value.

## Locked 2 Weapon Tier List 2026

The following tier list categorizes weapons based on their performance in both casual "Autos" and professional "Scrims." Some weapons excel in one but fail in the other, so choose according to your preferred game mode.

### S-Tier: The Unstoppable Meta
These weapons are currently the best in the game. If you roll one of these, do not spin off unless you are targeting a specific position-locked build.

*   **Black Hole Trap (Epic):** Excellent for offensive players. It auto-locks the ball to your feet, allowing you to bypass defenders with ease.

### B-Tier: Situationally Strong
*   **Power Shot (Rare):** Great for clearing the ball from the defensive third, but the long charge time makes it risky for strikers.

*   **Super Fast Scissors (Epic):** Provides some I-frames (invincibility frames) during the initial animation, useful for cutting inside the box.

### C-Tier: Niche or Underwhelming
*   **Metavision (Unique):** As discussed, **metavision locked 2** provides utility that is often overshadowed by more aggressive options.
*   **Perfect Counter (Unique):** High skill ceiling. It requires the opponent to tackle you at a specific time to proc, making it unreliable against smart defenders.
*   **Direct Shot (Common):** Once a meta-staple, it has fallen off due to the requirement of a perfect teammate pass.

## Advanced Mechanics: I-Frames and Stacking

To truly master the game beyond just having a good weapon like **metavision locked 2**, you must understand I-frames (Invincibility Frames). You can identify I-frames when the ball glows blue during an animation. During this period, the ball cannot be stolen. Weapons like Perfect Counter provide brief I-frames, which is why they are rated higher than "Terrible" tier weapons that leave the ball exposed.

Furthermore, stacking is the key to high-level scoring. A player can use a weapon like Riptide Curve or Power Shot and combine it with Emperor Impact to create a "Magna Shot." This combined power makes the ball travel at speeds that even the best goalkeepers using Primal Handstand struggle to react to.

## FAQ

**Q: Is Metavision Locked 2 better for defense or offense?**

A: It is technically a hybrid weapon, but it shines most in the midfield. The speed boost helps with transitions, and the trajectory view helps in intercepting long balls. However, it lacks the scoring power of Emperor Impact and the clearing power of Serpent.

**Q: What is the best weapon for a Goalkeeper?**

A: Primal Handstand is widely considered the best GK weapon. Its hitbox is massive, allowing you to block shots aimed at the "top bins" even if you are standing in the center of the goal. It is so powerful that it is often banned for non-GK players in competitive leagues.

**Q: Can I change my weapon after I've picked one?**

A: Yes, but it requires spending in-game currency to "spin" for a new one. Because the results are randomized based on rarity percentages, you should always ensure you have a "savings cushion" before trying to replace a decent weapon.

**Q: Why is Emperor Impact considered S-Tier?**

A: Emperor Impact is the most consistent scoring weapon in the game. It provides a massive power boost to your next shot regardless of your positioning, making it the most lethal tool for any player in the attacking third.**Q: Does Metavision protect against all tackles?**

A: No. While **metavision locked 2** prevents you from being ankle broken (tripped) by certain dribbling moves, you can still have the ball stolen via a standard M2 tackle or a well-timed slide if you do not have I-frames active.
