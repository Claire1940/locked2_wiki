During these frames, the ball glows blue, signifying that it cannot be stolen by any means.

This prevents "guaranteed" goals that would otherwise ruin the competitive balance of the game.

### Defensive Counter-Play
If you are facing a player using the **perfect counter locked 2**, the best strategy is patience.

2. **Hold M1:** Instead of committing to an M2 tackle, use M1 to maintain pressure without triggering the counter.
3. **Wait it Out:** After 1.5 seconds, the user is vulnerable and usually out of stamina from the failed activation.

**Q: Is Perfect Counter Locked 2 better than Emperor Impact?**

**Q: Can the I-frames from Perfect Counter be broken?**

A: No, when the ball is blue during the Zone state, it is physically impossible for another player to steal it. However, the state only lasts a few seconds, so you must move quickly.

**Q: Why can't I use Perfect Counter in the box during scrims?**

A: Most competitive leagues ban its use in the box because the I-frames prevent defenders from doing their job, making it an "un-counterable" path to the goal. Always check your league's specific rulebook.
