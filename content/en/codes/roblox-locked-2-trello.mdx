Once you have mastered the basics found on the **roblox locked 2 trello**, you must focus on the "mental" side of the game. The Trello board usually outlines the specific "Flow Triggers" for each weapon. Knowing these triggers allows you to play more intentionally and force your character into their peak performance state.
