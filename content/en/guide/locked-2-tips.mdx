## Mastering Flow Traits and Customization

One of the first decisions you face in the main menu lobby is selecting your Flow Trait. Unlike the previous game, these buffs have distinct multipliers and drain rates that can drastically alter your performance in 11v11 or 4v4 matches. Currently, the community meta revolves around two primary options: **Off-Ball** and **Burst**.

While many players initially gravitated toward Tenacity, testing has shown that Off-Ball is superior for competitive play. Tenacity claims to drain flow slower, but the actual multiplier is significantly weaker than Off-Ball. When using Off-Ball, you lose less flow specifically when you don't have the ball, which, in an 11v11 format, is roughly 90% of the match.

| Flow Trait | Best Used For | Key Advantage | Recommended Sub-Stats |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Off-Ball** | Midfielders / Defenders | High speed multiplier with long duration | Speed, Reach |
| **Burst** | Strikers / Wingers | Instant acceleration for goal-scoring | Strength, Reach |
| **Tenacity** | Casual Play | Passive flow retention | Technique |
| **Reach** | Center Backs | Increased interception radius | Strength |

> **💡 Tip:** Avoid using Technique as a sub-stat in the current 2026 meta. Most competitive players find that Strength or Reach provide much more tangible benefits during physical duels and interceptions.

## Height and Stat Percentages: Finding Your Build

Height in Locked 2 is no longer just a cosmetic choice or a simple speed-for-power trade-off. The 2026 update introduced visible percentage modifiers for every height, affecting cooldowns, stamina, and power. 

The most versatile height currently is **5'9"**, as it serves as the "all-rounder" build with the lowest natural cooldowns for weapons. However, your choice should ultimately reflect your specific role on the pitch.

| Height | Role Archetype | Primary Benefit | Trade-off |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **5'3"** | Speedster / Winger | Maximum agility and movement | Very low physical strength |
| **5'9"** | Midfield / All-Rounder | **Lowest weapon cooldowns** | Average in all categories |
| **6'0"** | Modern Striker | Balanced power and height | Slightly longer cooldowns |
| **6'3"** | Target Man / Defender | Maximum power and reach | Slowest movement speed |

Experimentation is key here. While a 6'3" striker with "Bunnies" was the meta in the past, Locked 2 allows for much more variety. A 6'0" striker using Formless or Big Bang Drive often has more options for creative playmaking than a pure power build.

## The Manual Bicycle Kick Mechanic

A subtle but game-changing addition in Locked 2 is the manual bicycle kick. Previously, this was often tied to specific weapons like Big Bang Drive. Now, it is a universal mechanic that can be toggled in your control settings.

To perform a manual bicycle kick, you must hold your backwards key (default **S**), charge your shot, jump, and release the charge while midair. This isn't just for style; the bicycle kick animation allows your character to reach heights significantly higher than a standard jump. This is incredibly useful for strikers trying to win headers or defenders clearing high balls.

> **⚠️ Warning:** If you are using the **Nagi** weapon, the manual bicycle kick can ruin your plays. Holding the backwards key while trying to trap or shoot with Nagi often triggers a bicycle kick animation that flings the ball backwards toward your own goal. Beginners should consider disabling this feature in the settings until their muscle memory is fully developed.

## Weapon Stacking: The Professional Meta

There is a common misconception that weapon stacking is a bug or an exploit. The developers have officially confirmed that weapon stacking is an intentional mechanic designed to let players combine the "best traits" of two different weapons.

When you stack weapons, the game takes the most advantageous stats from each. For example, if you stack **Riptide** (a curve-heavy weapon) with **Emperor** (a fast-charging, powerful weapon), the resulting shot will have the extreme curve of Riptide but the instant charge and increased velocity of Emperor.

| Recommended Stack | Primary Effect | Strategy |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Riptide + Emperor** | Magnus Kaiser Effect | Instant, high-speed curve shots from distance. |
| **Power Shot + Emperor** | Instant Railgun | Deletes the 5-second charge time of Power Shot. |
| **Direct Shot + Emperor** | Ultimate Volley | Maximum power for one-touch finishes. |
| **Formless + Dragon Header** | Aerial Dominance | High-altitude headers that are nearly impossible to block. |

Emperor is currently the most popular "base" for stacking because it lacks significant downsides and provides a massive utility boost to almost any unique weapon in the game.

## Private Server Commands and Multipliers

For those looking to host scrimmages or practice sessions, the private server (PS) UI has been completely revamped. You no longer need to memorize text commands; everything is handled via the **M** menu under the "Commands" tab.

The admin has total control over the environment, including field size and ball physics. If you want to simulate a more competitive environment, you should stick to the default multipliers, but for specialized training, these settings are invaluable.

| Setting | Default Value | Effect of Increasing |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Ball Gravity** | **7** | Makes the ball drop faster (heavier feel). |
| **Flow Charge** | 1x | Increases how quickly you enter Flow state. |
| **Field Size** | Large | Switches between 11v11 and 7v7/5v5 dimensions. |
| **Hitbox Multiplier** | 1x | Makes it easier (or harder) to make contact with the ball. |

## FAQ

**Q: What is the best height for a beginner in Locked 2?**

A: We recommend starting at **5'9"**. It provides a 100% balance across all stats and features the lowest weapon cooldowns, making it easier to practice different weapons frequently.

**Q: How do I unlock new maps for my private server?**

A: Maps are not purchased with Robux. Instead, you must earn in-game **Yen** by completing daily challenges, entering codes, and winning matches. Once you have enough Yen, visit the in-game store to purchase maps like the U20 Stadium or the Third Selection field.

**Q: Why does my ball fly into the stands when I try to shoot?**

A: This usually happens if the **Ball Gravity** multiplier in your private server has been set too low (e.g., 1 or 2). Ensure the gravity is set to the default value of **7** for standard gameplay.

**Q: Are there any specific locked 2 tips for improving my defense?**

A: Focus on your **Reach** sub-stat and consider using the **Off-Ball** flow trait. This combination allows you to maintain high speed while positioning yourself to intercept passes, which is much more effective than trying to chase down strikers with pure power.
