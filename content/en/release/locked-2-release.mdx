## Advanced Gameplay Mechanics

To excel in the competitive ranks, players must look beyond basic passing and shooting. The **locked 2 release** rewards those who can master the technical nuances of the game.

### The Ego System and Flow State
The "Ego" system is a meter that fills as you perform actions that align with your archetype's goals. For a striker, this means taking shots and beating defenders. For a defender, it means successful tackles and interceptions. Once the meter is full, you can activate your "Flow State."

*   **Increased Speed:** Movement speed is boosted by 15%.
*   **Skill Enhancements:** Your "Weapon" ability gains secondary effects (e.g., a shot may gain more curve).
*   **Stamina Recovery:** Entering Flow instantly restores a portion of your stamina.

### Dribbling and Ball Control
Ball control is more manual than in the previous game. You must now account for the ball's spin and the surface friction of the pitch. Preparing for the **locked 2 release** involved extensive testing of these physics to ensure that dribbling feels rewarding rather than automated. Players can perform "Perfect Touches" by timing their inputs exactly as the ball reaches their feet, allowing for immediate transitions into sprints or shots.

> **⚠️ Warning:** Over-dribbling will rapidly deplete your stamina. If your stamina hits zero, your movement speed is halved, and you lose the ability to use special skills until it regenerates.
