## Offensive Shooting Weapons

Scoring is the heart of the game, and the diversity of **locked 2 all weapons** in the offensive category allows for multiple scoring archetypes. From precision curves to raw power, these are the tools of a master striker.

### Emperor Impact and Magnus Shots
The **Emperor Impact** remains one of the most formidable weapons in the game. It is a high-velocity, long-range shot that requires no hold time, making it incredibly difficult for goalkeepers to react to. When combined correctly, players can execute a **Magnus shot**, which adds unpredictable movement to the ball's flight path.

### Riptide Curve
For players who prefer finesse over power, the **Riptide Curve** allows for manual control over the ball's direction. This is ideal for shooting around defenders or catching a goalkeeper out of position. It is important to note that the standard "Straight Shot" has been removed from the current version of the game, making curve mastery even more essential.

### Big Bang Drive
This is a specialized 180-degree shot. Its unique mechanic allows the player to control the height of the flight, making it a nightmare for defenders trying to time their jumps.

## Dribbling and Movement Weapons

Getting the ball into the final third requires more than just running. Movement-based **locked 2 all weapons** provide the agility needed to break ankles and maintain momentum.

*   **Super Fast Scissors:** A rapid dash with the ball. During this animation, the ball is essentially "locked" to the player and cannot be stolen, making it the perfect tool for bypassing aggressive defenders.
*   **Perfect Counter:** If an opponent attempts a steal, the Perfect Counter allows you to transition into a long, extended dribble, effectively turning their aggression against them.
*   **Immense Speed:** This skill provides three accelerating surges of momentum. It is best used for wingers looking to outrun the backline.
*   **Monster Lift:** A high-skill dribble that lifts the ball. Use this with caution, as it is one of the few dribbles where you can still lose possession if timed poorly.
*   **Stealthy Steps:** This allows a player to briefly turn invisible while gaining a burst of speed. It is the ultimate tool for "ghosting" into the box undetected.

## Defensive and Goalkeeping Mastery

A strong offense wins games, but a strong defense wins championships. Comparing **locked 2 all weapons** for defensive players reveals a focus on hitboxes and positioning.

### Stylish Reach and Serpent
**Stylish Reach** is a passive-active hybrid that extends your defensive range. By increasing your character's hitbox, you can intercept passes that would otherwise be out of reach. **Serpent**, on the other hand, is a defensive jump skill. It allows you to adjust the height of your jump mid-air, which is crucial for blocking shots like the Big Bang Drive.

### Goalkeeper Specials
Goalkeepers have access to unique weapons designed to keep the sheet clean:
1.  **Bear Claw:** Instead of simply deflecting the ball, this skill allows the keeper to snatch the ball out of the air, securing possession immediately.
2.  **Primal Handstand:** This skill provides a massive, temporary hitbox. It is best used during "clutch" moments when multiple shots are being fired in quick succession.

> **⚠️ Warning:** Defensive skills often have longer cooldowns than offensive ones. Misusing a "Stylish Reach" can leave a gap in your formation that skilled strikers will exploit.

## Tactical Passive Abilities

Some of the most powerful **locked 2 all weapons** aren't active attacks but tactical buffs that change how you perceive the game.

### Meta Vision
Meta Vision is arguably the most coveted skill in the game. When active, it provides:
*   **Trajectory Visualization:** See exactly where the ball is going.
*   **Speed Boost:** A significant increase in movement velocity.
*   **Ankle Break Immunity:** You cannot be tripped or stunned by dribbling moves.

### Black Hole Trap and Bunny
**Black Hole Trap** gives you the supernatural ability to catch the ball mid-air and hold it for a brief moment, allowing you to settle the play before looking for a pass. **Bunny** is a simple but effective buff that ensures your next jump reaches a much higher peak, which is essential for both offensive and defensive headers.
