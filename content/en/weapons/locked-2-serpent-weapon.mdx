## Mastering the "Beam" and Scoring Techniques

Scoring with the Serpent requires a combination of timing and positioning. The "Beam" is the signature move for this weapon class, characterized by a straight, high-velocity shot that is difficult for goalkeepers to track. In 2026, the most effective way to utilize the beam is through the "Top Right" strategy.

1.  **Build Momentum:** Drive toward the corner of the penalty box to draw the defender out.
2.  **Check Composure:** Ensure your stamina bar is at least 60% to maintain maximum shot accuracy.
3.  **The Release:** Aim for the far post (Top Right or Top Left). The Serpent's natural curve will pull the ball away from the keeper's reach.

> **Warning:** Overusing the beam can lead to rapid stamina depletion. Always balance your high-powered shots with short, tactical passes to keep the defense guessing.
