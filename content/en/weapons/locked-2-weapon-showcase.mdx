Common and Rare weapons form the foundation of most early-game builds. A notable change in 2026 is the rework of **Direct Shot**, which now features a "green timing" mechanic. If you release the input exactly as the prompt procs, the shot power increases significantly, even if it isn't coming off a direct pass.

## Legendary Weapons: The Meta-Definers

Legendary weapons are rare drops that can shift the tide of a match. In 2026, **Immense Speed** (Chigiri’s signature) has seen a significant rework. Instead of a flat speed boost, it now ramps up exponentially over time. This makes it less effective for short bursts but devastating for long-distance sprints down the wing.

The **Riptide Curve** also remains a top-tier choice for playmakers. It allows for extreme ball curving on both passes and grounded "grass-cutter" shots. Players can toggle the direction of the curve mid-play, making it nearly impossible for goalkeepers to predict the trajectory of a long-range effort.

### Dragon Header Mechanics
The **Dragon Header** is one of the most difficult weapons to master in this **locked 2 weapon showcase**. It cannot be used while your character is already mid-air; instead, it must be activated from the ground to trigger the leaping animation. The height of the shot is determined by the ball's position on the Y-axis at the moment of impact. While the visual "dragon" effect has been toned down for performance, the shot speed remains among the fastest in the game.

## Unique Weapons: Peak Performance

Unique weapons represent the pinnacle of ability power. These are often tied to specific character archetypes and have the longest cooldowns to prevent spamming. **Big Bang Drive**, the evolved version of the old "Formless" style, is the most controversial addition in 2026. It features a long wind-up animation where the ball defies gravity and stays afloat with the player.

| Unique Weapon | Cooldown | Description |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Big Bang Drive** | 120 Seconds | Gravity-based aerial shot; best used for bicycle kicks off passes. |
| **Emperor** | 100 Seconds | Grants an instant charge-up for any shot type; pure offensive power. |
| **Serpent** | 150 Seconds | Specialized air-ball weapon; can be used mid-air for long-range snipes. |

**Serpent** is currently the weapon with the longest cooldown in the game. It is designed specifically to punish teams that allow the ball to linger in the air. Unlike many other high-tier weapons, Serpent can be activated while jumping, allowing for mid-air adjustments that catch defenders off-guard.

> **⚠️ Warning:** Big Bang Drive is no longer an "instant score" button. The long animation gives defenders ample time to react and block the shot if you use it predictably.

## Character Height and Weapon Scaling

A hidden mechanic often overlooked in a standard **locked 2 weapon showcase** is how character height affects weapon performance. In the 2026 update, physics play a larger role in ability effectiveness. 

1. **Short Heights (5'3" - 5'7"):** These characters benefit most from **Immense Speed** and **Bunny**. The lower center of gravity allows for faster acceleration and higher relative verticality.
2. **Tall Heights (6'0" - 6'3"):** These builds scale better with **Power Shot** and **Dragon Header**. The natural reach increases the effective hitbox of defensive weapons like **Primal Handstand**.

## Building the Ultimate Loadout

When selecting weapons from this **locked 2 weapon showcase**, synergy is more important than raw rarity. A common mistake is equipping two high-cooldown unique weapons, leaving you with no utility for most of the match.

*   **The Finisher Build:** Pair **Emperor** with **Direct Shot**. Use Emperor for the initial burst and Direct Shot for follow-up opportunities when the unique ability is on cooldown.
*   **The Wing Wizard:** Combine **Immense Speed** with **Riptide Curve**. Use your speed to beat the fullback and the curve to send a perfect cross to your striker.
*   **The Iron Wall:** Equip **Primal Handstand** and **Bearclaw**. This setup makes you an impenetrable force in the box, capable of stopping both grounded shots and aerial headers.

As of April 2026, **Meta Vision** and **King Chops** are currently undergoing reworks and are not available for standard play. Developers have hinted that these will return with entirely new mechanics to fit the updated physics engine.

## FAQ

**Q: What is the best weapon for a beginner in Locked 2?**

A: Direct Shot is the best starting weapon. It teaches you the "green timing" mechanic which is fundamental to high-level play, and it has a relatively low cooldown compared to Legendary options.

**Q: Can I use Dragon Header while jumping?**

A: No. Unlike the Serpent weapon, Dragon Header must be activated while your feet are on the ground. It will then trigger a specialized leaping animation to hit the ball.

**Q: How do I get Unique weapons in 2026?**

A: Unique weapons are obtained through the in-game economy system or rare drops. They are the hardest to acquire and often require significant playtime or luck during rolls.

**Q: Does Stealthy Steps make me completely invisible?**

A: Not entirely. While you are much harder to see, there is a slight distortion effect. Observant players can still track your movement if they are looking closely, so use it strategically rather than relying on it for total concealment.
