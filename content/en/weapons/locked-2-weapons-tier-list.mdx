## Deep Dive: S-Tier Weapons

### Chameleon Copy (The Reo Mikage Special)
The standout addition in 2026 is the Chameleon Copy. This weapon is unique because its functionality changes based on whether you have individual possession of the ball. It is a "copycat" style weapon that mimics the abilities of other players currently on the field.

*   **Offensive Use:** If you have the ball and activate Chameleon Copy, you will receive an offensive weapon (like Bunnies or Power Shot) stolen from an opponent or teammate.
*   **Defensive Use:** If you do not have the ball, activating the weapon provides a defensive tool, such as Stylish Reach.

This versatility makes it the most powerful tool in the **locked 2 weapons tier list**, though it requires high game sense to use effectively. You can only copy weapons that are actually present in your current match; if no one has a specific high-tier weapon equipped, you cannot copy it.

## Understanding Weapon Rarity and Economy

In 2026, the economy in Locked 2 is quite stringent. Players earn roughly 750 to 1,900 currency per match depending on their performance and MVP status. Because of this, rolling for new weapons must be done carefully.

The game features a "Weapon Filter" system in the customization tab. This is a vital quality-of-life feature that prevents you from accidentally overwriting a high-tier weapon.

1.  **Open Customization:** Navigate to the Weapon tab.
2.  **Set Filters:** Tick the boxes for "Legendary" and "Unique."
3.  **Confirmation Pop-ups:** With these filters active, the game will ask "Are you sure you want to roll?" if you attempt to replace a high-rarity weapon.
4.  **Avoid Accidental Rolls:** Never tick "Common" or "Rare" in the filter if you are hunting for top-tier gear, as this will remove the safety prompt for those lower rarities.

> **Warning:** Currently, there is a significant "money lack" in the game's release state. Do not waste your currency rolling for a new weapon if you already have an A-Tier option like Stylish Reach or Power Shot until you have a large surplus of cash.

## Strategies for Ranking Up

To effectively move up the **locked 2 weapons tier list** in terms of your own player skill, you must understand cooldown management. Cooldowns in Locked 2 typically range from 60 to 80 seconds. In a standard match, you may only get to use your weapon ability 3 or 4 times.

*   **Sync with Teammates:** Do not pop your offensive weapon at the same time as your co-striker. Stagger your abilities to ensure your team always has a "power play" available.
*   **Identify the Opposition:** Before the match starts, look at the weapons your opponents are carrying. If you see a Chameleon user, be aware that they can use your own weapon against you.
*   **The I-Frame Meta:** Some weapons provide frames of invincibility (I-frames). Learning the exact timing of when these frames end is crucial for high-level competitive play.

## FAQ

**Q: What is the rarest weapon in the locked 2 weapons tier list?**

A: Currently, Chameleon Copy and specific "Unique" variants are the rarest. These have a very low roll percentage and often require a significant amount of currency or luck to obtain.

**Q: How does Chameleon Copy decide which weapon to mimic?**

A: It is based on the other players in the server. If you activate it while you have the ball, it randomly selects an offensive weapon from another player on the field. If you don't have the ball, it selects a defensive one.

**Q: Is there a way to get more money fast in Locked 2?**

A: The best way to earn currency in 2026 is by achieving a high match rating. Scoring goals, providing assists, and winning as the MVP can grant you up to 1.9k per match, whereas a standard loss or low-participation game might only grant 750.

**Q: Can I use Chameleon Copy in private servers?**

A: Yes, it works in private servers, and some servers may have multipliers that affect the cooldown times, though the base cooldown is roughly 60-80 seconds depending on the weapon copied.
