## Offensive Weapons: Striker Essentials

The primary role of any striker is to find the back of the net, and the offensive **locked 2 weapons** are designed to make that task easier—or at least more spectacular. Shooting in this game isn't just about power; it is about trajectory, timing, and outsmarting the goalkeeper.

### Long-Range and Curve Shots

One of the most versatile tools in a striker's kit is the **Riptide Curve**. This weapon allows for significant lateral movement of the ball mid-flight, which can be manually controlled by the player. It is particularly effective against goalkeepers who over-commit to one side of the net. However, players should note that the traditional "Straight Shot" has been phased out in recent updates, making curve mastery even more vital.

For those who prefer raw speed, the **Emperor Impact** stands out. This is a lightning-fast, long-range projectile that requires no charge-up time. When executed correctly, it can be combined with other mechanics to create a "Magnus Shot," a terrifyingly fast strike that is almost impossible to track with the naked eye.

### Aerial Dominance: Headers and Volleys

Not every goal is scored with the feet. The **Dragon Header** is a premier offensive weapon that sees the player launch themselves forward with immense momentum to meet the ball. Similarly, the **Direct Shot** rewards team play; by shooting immediately off a teammate's pass, the resulting strike gains a significant speed boost, punishing defenders who leave gaps in their formation.

## Dribbling and Mobility Weapons

Moving the ball down the field requires more than just sprinting. The movement-based **locked 2 weapons** focus on agility, invisibility, and ball retention.

### Elite Dribbling Skills

The **Super Fast Scissors** is a mandatory pick for wingers. During this short dash, the ball is essentially "locked" to the player, making it impossible for defenders to steal it through conventional means. If you find yourself cornered, the **Monster Lift** offers a creative escape by flicking the ball over an opponent, though it carries the risk of losing possession if timed poorly.

For those who value unpredictability, **Stealthy Steps** provides a momentary burst of speed while turning the player invisible. This is the ultimate tool for "ghosting" behind a defensive line to receive a through-ball.

| Skill Name | Movement Type | Defensive Counter | Skill Gap |

## Defensive Mastery and Goalkeeping

A strong offense wins games, but a legendary defense wins championships. The defensive **locked 2 weapons** focus on expanding hitboxes and neutralizing the striker's advantages.

### The Defensive Wall

**Stylish Reach** is perhaps the most common defensive tool seen in high-level play. It passively extends the player's defensive range, effectively giving them a larger hitbox for interceptions and tackles. When paired with **Serpent**, which allows for adjustable jump heights, a defender can shut down both ground and air attacks with ease.

For the last line of defense, goalkeepers have access to specialized weapons:
1.  **Bear Claw:** Instead of merely deflecting the ball, this skill allows the GK to snatch the ball out of the air, immediately ending the opponent's offensive pressure.
2.  **Primal Handstand:** This creates a massive, temporary hitbox at the feet of the goalkeeper, ideal for stopping low-driven shots or "Power Shots" at close range.

## Tactical Weapons and Meta Vision

Some **locked 2 weapons** don't fit into a simple category because they alter the fundamental rules of the game. **Meta Vision** is the most prominent example. When activated, the player gains a speed boost and can see the projected trajectory of the ball. Most importantly, Meta Vision makes the player immune to "Ankle Breaks," a common mechanic used by high-tier dribblers to stun defenders.

Another tactical marvel is the **Black Hole Trap**. This allows a player to catch a ball mid-air and hold it momentarily, killing all momentum. It is the perfect counter to high-speed crosses, allowing a team to reset their formation and start a controlled counter-attack.

> **Tip:** Use the **Bunny** skill before attempting a Black Hole Trap on a high ball to ensure you reach the apex of the ball's flight path before your opponent.

## FAQ

**Q: What are the best locked 2 weapons for a beginner?**

A: For beginners, **Stylish Reach** and **Power Shot** are highly recommended. Stylish Reach is a passive buff that helps you defend more effectively without complex inputs, while Power Shot provides a reliable way to score goals while you learn the more advanced mechanics like the Riptide Curve.

**Q: Can I use multiple weapons at once?**

A: Yes, players can equip a set of skills, but they often share a cooldown or energy pool. Managing your "Stamina" or "Aura" is key to ensuring you have your weapons available during the most critical moments of the match.

**Q: How do I perform a Magnus Shot in Locked 2?**

A: A Magnus Shot is an advanced technique created by combining the **Emperor Impact** with specific directional inputs and timing. It results in a shot that has both the extreme velocity of the Emperor Impact and the unpredictable movement of a curve shot.

**Q: Did they remove the Straight Shot weapon?**

A: Yes, as of the 2026 updates, the traditional Straight Shot has been removed to encourage players to use more technical skills like the Riptide Curve or the Big Bang Drive. This change was intended to increase the skill ceiling for strikers.
